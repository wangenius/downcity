---
title: Logging and Usage
description: How to record AI calls by user, studio, and model.
---

Downcity does not decide where your logs live, but hooks give you one consistent integration point.

## Recommended fields

- user_id
- studio_id
- model
- started_at
- ended_at
- status
- error

## Where to record

```ts
ai.hook.after(async (ctx) => {
  await logService.write({
    user_id: ctx.user?.user_id,
    studio_id: ctx.studio?.studio_id,
    model: ctx.variant?.id,
    model_meta: ctx.variant?.meta,
    started_at: ctx.started_at,
    ended_at: ctx.ended_at,
  });
});
```

Once many studios share one City, usage data becomes centralized by default, which makes cost analysis and business decisions much easier later.
